Greg Knese's Home Page

I'm a Professor of Mathematics at Washington University in St. Louis.
I am Director of Graduate Studies in the Department of Mathematics since 2022.
I was previously at the University of Alabama (2010-2013) and UC Irvine (2007-2010).
You can email me at geknese shift2 wustl dot edu.
My office is 214 Cupples I
My CV
ORCID arXiv MathSciNet Google scholar

Research

I am interested in operator theory, complex analysis,
stable polynomials, and reproducing kernel Hilbert spaces
with applications to probability and theoretical computer science.

Editorial work

Handling editor for Bulletin of the London Math Society and Journal of the London Math Society
